Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Cipher Social Engine (“the App”) is a software tool created by CipherTech to help users automate, schedule, and enhance social media posting. This policy explains how we handle data when you connect your Facebook or Instagram account.

Data We Collect

  • Basic Facebook Page information (name, ID, profile picture)
  • Permissions you grant the App (manage page content, read insights, etc.)
  • Posts or comments that the App creates or moderates on your behalf

How Data Is Used

  • To publish posts you schedule
  • To analyze engagement
  • To allow Cipher AI to assist with comments or messaging (if enabled)
  • To improve app functionality and performance

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your data. Data is only shared with:

  • Meta/Facebook APIs (required for posting)
  • Secure service providers hosting the App (e.g., Vercel, Firebase)

Data Storage

Data is stored securely and only for as long as necessary to operate the App. You may request full deletion at any time.
