Data Deletion Instructions

If you have authorized Cipher Social Engine to access your Facebook account and wish to delete your data, follow the steps below.

Delete App Data via Facebook

  1. Go to Facebook.com
  2. Open Settings & Privacy
  3. Go to Settings
  4. Select Business Integrations
  5. Find Cipher Social Engine
  6. Click Remove

This removes the App and automatically deletes all associated data stored by Facebook.

Request Manual Deletion

If you want your information deleted from CipherTech’s servers as well, email:
